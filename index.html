Because sex is something we consider as very private, speaking about sexual practices tends to nourish our fantasies. You may have wondered what the small secret pleasures of your friends, neighbors, or even boss might be. The bad news – though it could be considered good after all – is that unless you try questioning them yourself, you'll never know much more. This is why many clichés are still going strong. Are there really fewer homosexuals amongst Catholics? Is sodomy more frequent among left-wingers or right-wingers? Go ahead and test your a priori on sex with total impunity.

Sexuality and religion

Sodom and Gomorra, where do we stand ?

Not so much of a difference ! The percentage of non-religious people who have already had sexual intercourse with someone of the same sex is 16%. This percentage is the same for individuals belonging to a religion other than Catholicism.

As for Catholics, they seem to be less tempted by homosexual experiences, even though the difference is hardly significant – 12% of Catholics have already had an experience with someone of the same sex. Only four fewer percentage points than the rest of the population !

More important than religious affiliations, it is therefore the actual practising of one's religion which is crucial in explaining one's sexual habits. 63% of practicing Catholics have never engaged in sodomy (even with someone of the same sex) as opposed to 46% for the rest of the population

Oral sex... gender oriented ?

He/She licks me a little, a lot ... not at all

We have to admit, in our collective imagination, blow jobs are more frequent than cunnilingus (let alone in cinema)... But in fact ? Who actually performs the most oral sex ? Men or women ?

Apparently, women go into more detail than men when talking about sex with their friends… It's rare to hear a man describe exactly what he did the night before with his partner. Besides, only too seldom do we hear a man boasting about his efforts to please his partner…

But in fact a higher proportion of men (49%) than women (36%) licks or sucks their partner's genitals. Conversely, 15% of women declare never doing it, as opposed to only 11% for men. When you get down to it, it's clear, whatever your partner's gender, sex treats are good, indulge in them !

And your level of education, what does it change?

You now know that a greater number of men than women declare often sucking or licking their partner's genitals. But if we delve a little deeper, can we see differences according to social groups ? Okay, we'll give you a hint, college degrees have something to do with it.

Your level of education indeed has an influence on the frequency of oral sex… For whom is this influence the strongest ? Women ! Among men with a high school diploma up to a college education, 48% declare often sucking or licking their partner's genitals. Among those who don't have a high school diploma, this percentage goes up to 50%. Conversely, 13% of the former declare never doing it, as opposed to 9% for the latter. The difference there isn't enormous, and is greater for women.

Among women, the effect of their education level is the opposite. Those with a high school diploma or a higher degree are more likely to use their mouths often and less likely to never use them. What does this opposite effect between men and women convey? Education contributes to narrowing the gap in terms of sexual practices between genders, so that everyone can enjoy the lingual talents of the opposite sex! Politics free
of complexes

... and politicized love

As you've seen earlier, sexual tastes vary according to religious affiliations. What about the effect of political affiliations ?

Is political conservatism synonymous with sexual conservatism ? Well, yes and no. It is true that sodomy among leftists is a bit more frequent than among right-wingers: 53% of the electorate of the French Socialist Party and 51% of ecologists declare having already tried anal penetration as opposed to 50% for the electors of the UMP (the right wing party of Nicolas Sarkozy) and 49% of the electors of the Modem-UDI (right center). Those latter are also the most reticent about homosexual relations. Thus, 10% of the UMP electorate declare having experienced a same sex relationship, as opposed to 15% for the PS electorate.

But it is actually at the extremes of the political spectrum that the party gets the wildest. The Front de Gauche (a far left movement) and the Front National (far right) thus win the prize of the most sexually uninhibited members, with respectively 59% and 57% of the electorate having already tried sodomy, which is on average 8 points percentage points more than the more moderate electors! The same U-shaped scheme is found again concerning sexual relations with more than two people at once. The Front National and Front de Gauche are in the lead, with respectively 22% and 21% of their electors having already given their love to several people at the same time. There you have it -- some common values!

Recipes for satisfaction

The law of large numbers...
even for sexual partners ?

New is always better ! The unknown, that dazzling uncertainty... some insist that pleasure can only increase with new experiences and new encounters. Though at the same time, those who know but few, know them better! Why would the satisfaction not be more intense for those having known only a few bed partners ?

If we compare lovebirds to the hot-to-trot, which are the most sexually satisfied ? Congratulations to the lovebirds ! Among those who are little or not satisfied with their sexual lives, the least unsatisfied are those who have had only one partner. Among the steady lovers, 1 in 4 is little satisfied or not satisfied at all, as opposed to 1 in 3 for the hot-to-trot who have had four or five different partners!

Yet we notice that once there is more than one partner, the dissatisfaction rates are more or less the same, whether it be a case of two or eighteen partners! How can we explain this? The special value attributed to a first love, a total bonding with the significant other? On the other hand, do we become increasingly demanding when our prospects are multiplied? The answer is in your hands!

Moving in together... a big step! After one or more years living with roommates, doing pretty much whatever you want, is it that simple to suddenly live together, and, more importantly, to still be as satisfied with your sex life now that those bubbly Are you coming over to my place tonight? have disappeared? On the other hand, one's place becomes the other's place, and conversely, it's rather appealing after all...

21% of cohabiting couples are little or not satisfied with their sex life...

1 in 3 member of a cohabiting couple is very satisfied with their sex life...

...it is the case for only 14% of non-cohabiting couples

...whereas there is 1 in 2 among non-cohabiting couples

These non-cohabiting couples who are more satisfied than the others — our friends the reasearchers call them living apart together (LAT ) — are very nice, but what about single people ? Among couples (LAT or not), one out of five respondents are little or not satisfied. Among single people — whether confirmed in their singlehood or having already been in a relationship — we go up to one out of two ! The mutual trust and shared intimacy we experience with the same familiar sexual partner seems to be essential to our well-being. People in a relationship claim to be far less unsatisfied than single people. So, to live happily, live apart... but in a relationship !

Getting old... still getting laid ?

When sex has (almost) no age

Old, me? I can still make love twice in a row. Once in winter, and once in summer.

Alfred Capus

Are Grandma and Grandpa still able to have a go at it ? Against all odds, yes ! The share of respondents with a moderate but steady sexual activity (around once a week) is the same for all ages: 45% (click on the graph's legend). However, those under 25 are in the lead, with 15% of them who make love every other day. For those over 65 enjoying such a pace, the share goes down to... 2%.

No reason to panic however ! between 50 and 65, there is still 1 in 4 respondents with a frequency of at least twice a week . Better yet, by cumulating the respondents, we get that more than half of those over 65 who give a response other than “never”… Good news, then, we can still enjoy sex when we're old, but with a bit of moderation!
